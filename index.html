Moonbus Technologies

We are a trade and transportation technology company in Houston, TX. While the world has been brought together completely virtually, there is still a lot more needed to done to achieve that level accessibility physically. Moonbus aims to bridge this virtual-physical gap. Efficient flow of goods, ideas and peoples is a prerequisite for civilization-building. Our long-term vision is to close this gap through a two-staged strategy that involves building new software systems for greater integration followed by physical infrastructure.
